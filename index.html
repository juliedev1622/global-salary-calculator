What is Compare Global Salary Calculator?

This tool provides a quick estimate of salary comparison between different countries for outsourcing options, helping businesses save on costs by hiring offshore employees.

How does this Compare Global Salary Calculator work?

By selecting a data source, position, business country, and offshoring country, this calculator compares the average salaries and provides an estimated savings figure.

How to use this Compare Global Salary Calculator?

Select the source, position, country of business, and offshoring country from the dropdowns, and then click 'Calculate' to view the estimated savings.
